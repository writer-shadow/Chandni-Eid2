Eid Mubarak, my dearest one,
My radiant moon, my morning sun,
This holy day brings joy anew,
But my greatest blessing, is you.

Your eyes outshine the festive lights,
A beacon through the darkest nights,
With every blink, they softly gleam,
And pull me deeper in a dream.

I prayed for peace, I prayed for grace,
And then I saw your lovely face,
Allah heard my quiet plea,
And sent You down to me.

Your laughter is a sweet refrain,
Like music dancing in the rain,
It fills my heart with boundless cheer,
And makes the shadows disappear.

Your kindness is a quiet stream,
More precious than a fleeting dream,
You treat the world with such sweet care,
A soul so gentle and so rare.

The henna on your hands so fine,
A work of art, a sweet design,
But even art could never trace,
The pure perfection of your face.

I wish you joy, I wish you peace,
I wish your happiness won't cease,
May Allah bless your every step,
And guard the promises we've kept.

I wish you health, I wish you light,
To guide you through the darkest night,
May every sorrow pass you by,
And wipe the tears from your sweet eye.

May every prayer you whisper low,
Take root and beautifully grow,
May every dream you hold inside,
Become a reality, open wide.

You are the calm within my storm,
The fire that keeps my spirit warm,
The gentle touch that heals my soul,
The missing piece that makes me whole.

Your grace is like a blooming rose,
That ever-gently, softly grows,
With every petal, soft and sweet,
You make my life so quite complete.

You have a heart of purest gold,
A wondrous story to be told,
With endless depth and loving might,
You make the heavy burdens light.

Your elegance is plain to see,
A quiet, royal majesty,
You walk with such a regal flair,
And turn heads simply standing there.

May all your days be filled with sweet,
And joyful moments when we meet,
May every morning bring you hope,
And give you strength to climb and cope.

You are my joy, my truest friend,
A beautiful story without an end,
Let festive joys fill every space,
Reflected in your gorgeous face.
